Historical Evolution: A Journey Through Time

1966

ELIZA - The First Chatbot

MIT's Joseph Weizenbaum created ELIZA, a landmark program that simulated conversation using pattern matching, fooling some users into believing it was human.

1997

Deep Blue Defeats Kasparov

IBM's chess-playing computer defeated the world champion, showcasing AI's rapidly growing computational power to the public.

2011

Watson Wins Jeopardy!

IBM's question-answering system demonstrated advanced natural language understanding by competing against and winning on the popular quiz show.

2022

The Dawn of Mainstream Generative AI

The public release of models like ChatGPT marked a watershed moment, making generative AI widely accessible and showcasing its ability to produce human-like text.
